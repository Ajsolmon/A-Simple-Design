Yam and Egg Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Tuber Yam
  • 7 eggs
  • 3 fresh tomatoes diced
  • 4 fresh pepper diced
  • 1 onions diced
  • 1 cksp veg oil
  • to taste salt
  • 1 maggi cube
  • 1 green bell pepper diced

Instructions

  • Peel and wash yam and per boil with dash of salt once its soft and tender turn off heat, drain and turn off heat.
  • Break eggs in a bowl and add apinch of salt and whisk properly; heat up frying pan and once it hot add veg oil diced onions and stir in tomatoes, salt, maggi and pepper fry for 7mins
  • Pour egg into the frying pan and scramble everything together, finally add the green bell pepper and scramble everything together. turn off heat immediately and serve with boiled yam
